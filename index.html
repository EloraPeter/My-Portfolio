About Me

I’m Elora, a Software Engineering student and Web Developer based in Delta State, Nigeria. With extensive experience in designing and deploying professional websites, I specialize in crafting visually appealing and functional digital solutions for startups and brands.

As the founder of Elora Tech Institute (ETI), I lead a tech academy that trains students in front-end development, automation, and digital skills. My 12-week program has empowered countless learners to build real-world projects hosted on GitHub.

My technical skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress, Node.js, and automation with Puppeteer. I’m also proficient in branding, creating logos, CVs, and ad campaigns that combine design and strategy.

Currently, I manage a team developing scalable solutions, including a certification system and an online learning platform. My mission is to empower through education and make the web more accessible and beautiful.
